Phishing Email Analysis Report

Objective

The objective of this task is to analyze a sample phishing email and identify common phishing indicators.

Phishing Indicators Found

1. Fake Sender Email

The sender email address is support@paypa1-security.com. It is not the official PayPal domain and uses the number 1 instead of the letter l, making it look legitimate.

2. Urgent Subject Line

The subject says that the account will be suspended. This creates fear and pressures the user to act immediately.

3. Suspicious Link

The email contains the link:
http://paypal-login-security.xyz/verify

This is not an official PayPal website and is likely intended to steal user credentials.

4. Generic Greeting

The email starts with Dear Customer instead of addressing the recipient by name.

5. Threatening Language

The email warns that the account will be permanently suspended if no action is taken within 24 hours.

6. Email Header Analysis

The email header was not available, so header analysis could not be performed.

Conclusion

This email shows multiple phishing characteristics, including a fake sender address, a suspicious URL, urgent language, and a generic greeting. These indicators strongly suggest that it is a phishing email designed to trick users into revealing sensitive information.
